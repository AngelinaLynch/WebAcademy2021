What is favicon?

Favicon is the little icon next to the website name. For example youtubes Favicon is the little white and red play button, google classroom is the person icon. Another example is google their favicon is a multicolored G. All favicons are usually unique and thats how people brand their website. If you just saw the icons for some websites we use everyday you will already know what website it is. Favicons give some originality to everyones website.


How are favicons created?

Favicons can designed with a editor by different types of editors. Once people are done with this they can go on "favicon.com" to make there design come to life. After this you can download it to your computer and then add it into your html.

How did I create the favicon for this site?

Favicons are created by using an editor. But before I did all of that I sketched my design out. After I was satisfied with my design I went onto code.org and did my designs in code. I did 32x32 and 24 bits per pixel.I used the codes " FFB6C1 " for light pink and "FF1493" for the darker pink. Every color has different codes you can program into the boxes.After you create your design in color code by code you can downloade it and use it later.
